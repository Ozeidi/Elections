Have you ever been frustrated about buying a car!

This application is based on  ~160,000 car listings scrapped from gumtree.com.au.

It is designed to help you in taking data-driven decsion on What car to buy, where to find it, when to buy it and how much is it actualy worth compared to the overal market price?

To explore more details about the listings, have a look at the "Explorer" Tab.

Enjoy using the app and find your dream car!
